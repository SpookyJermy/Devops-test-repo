Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Fill out the form above and our team will contact you. We'll discuss your experience level and help you find the perfect expedition.

No! We welcome beginners. Our structured training programs will prepare you for any adventure at your pace and skill level.

Membership plans start at $99/month. Expedition costs vary by destination and difficulty. We offer flexible payment options.

We run expeditions year-round. Check our events calendar or contact us directly to find dates that work for you.
